# Tulpa Document Style

## Required Vocabulary

Use the product terms defined in [AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md).
Do not replace these with employment-oriented wording for variation alone.

- `稼働記録`
- `作業開始`
- `作業終了`
- `稼働時間`
- `稼働確認`
- `稼働報告`

## Product Framing

- Describe Tulpa as an OSS for connecting freelance engineers and agents through project-centered data.
- Treat AI and Slack as optional features, not prerequisites.
- Keep the focus on project-centered data, operational clarity, and better understanding between engineers, agents, and clients.

## Tone

- Prefer plain, direct Japanese over inflated marketing language.
- Keep the author's intent visible; do not sand off strong claims that are central to the document.
- Prefer precise wording over broad synonym variety.

## Editing Priorities

1. Accuracy
2. Terminology consistency
3. Structural clarity
4. Removal of accidental repetition
5. Rhythm and polish
